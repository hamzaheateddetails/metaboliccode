Want to Take Control of Your Health?
Start With Metabolic Code®

As a Lifestyle Transformation brand, we address the common issues that people deal with related to self care and an aspirational way of living. The Metabolic Code creates a common language between practitioner and patient in helping to understand and change their health.

Pioneering Integrative Medicine for over 30 years

A comprehensive approach that blends the best of eastern and western medicine into one system made up of lifestyle, lab testing, supplements and dietary recommendations.

Metabolic Code® has been evolving over a 30-year period. Jim LaValle, RPh, CCN, has pioneered and applied this unique and highly effective preventive approach to integrative health care to over 10,000 patients. His clinic, LaValle Metabolic Institute is recognized as one of the leading practices in integrative medicine in the world. He has demonstrated that patients can significantly improve their health through application of well chosen, high quality supplements and dietary planning driven by specific lab bio-markers testing and assessment methods. 

In addition, his approach is being validated and enhanced by the participation of Dr. Andrew Heyman, who was instrumental in the creation of the Program for Integrative and Metabolic Medicine at George Washington University, members of the Consortium, and the prestigious MHR Science Advisory Board.

Our Goal 

The goal of the Metabolic Code is to improve an individual’s lab values and most importantly the way the person feels. The Metabolic Code breaks down complex health issues and organizes them in a way that allows healthcare professionals and their patients to understand and gain insight into their current health. It can be applied in management of chronic illnesses and health conditions as well. A healthcare professional can help someone dealing with cardiovascular disease and diabetes, or they can help them to manage their weight loss when other methods have failed. The Metabolic Code protocol is the result of a collaboration with some of the leading healthcare professionals within the integrative health and lifestyle medicine space.

We strive to be on the forefront of research and cutting edge science to bring our patients and partners the best.

3 Easy Steps to Better Health & Vitality 

The Metabolic Code is a proprietary approach to clinical assessment and treatment planning implemented as a component of all treatment programming where appropriate within medical and non-medical services. We want to keep things simple for practitioners and patients so our system is based on a three step approach.

1. Take Lifestyle Assessment

The Metabolic Code protocol is unique in that it views metabolism as a sum total of all the factors that influence health throughout one’s life. These factors include stress, environment, spirituality, prescription drugs, and diet. The protocol begins with an extensive web-based lifestyle questionnaire as well as a well crafted and targeted blood panel. We analyze traditional measures, as well as many areas such as toxic levels, food allergies, drug-nutrient depletions and how all of these factors are interrelated.
Start Your Journey

2. Get Your Personalized Report

Based on the results from your assessment, the person receives a personalized health assessment using our TRIAD system. This gives the doctor and patient an individual “road map” to significantly enhance their health and vitality. This “road map” will include what foods to eat and quality nutritional supplements. The focus is on improving the TRIADS and also establishing a healthy lifestyle long term to help support your goals.
Get a Sample Report

3. Follow Your Program

One key factor of short and long term success is a person's diet. Based on their assessment everyone receives a 3-phased Metabolic Code Diet system which helps to reset and restore the metabolic systems throughout the body. It focuses on reducing inflammation as well as blood sugar levels and really teaches individuals how to establish a healthy new lifestyle.
